Seapine Brewing Company

Founded in 2011 in a small 750 square foot space on 4th ave, Seapine has grown to produce some of the most consistent beers in the Pacific Northwest. In 2015, with production needs ramping up, Seapine relocated about a mile away from its first home to a new 6,000 square foot operation on Utah Ave, continuing to churn out some amazing brews. With a great space and friendly staff, this is definitely a spot to check out in Seattle.
