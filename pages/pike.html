Pike Brewing Company

Founded in 1989 by Charles and Rose Ann Finkel, Pike Brewing Company has become a staple of many locals and tourists that visit the Pike Place Market. Offering a family friendly spot to grab some food and a few good drinks, Pike offers an extremely welcoming pub as well as a more upscale eating experience in the upstairs shootoff, Tankard & Tun.
